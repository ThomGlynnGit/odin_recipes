Broad Bean Risotto

Description

This recipe creates a lovely broad bean risotto. Recipe is for 2 people.

Ingredients
  • 400g broad beans
  • 500g risotto rice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 300g Spinach/li>
Method
  1. Cook a risotto how you would usually cook a risotto.
  2. Blend spinach and garlic with cream and cream cheese or something.
  3. Add beans yum done.
