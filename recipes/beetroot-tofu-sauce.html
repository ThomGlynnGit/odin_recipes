Broad Bean Risotto

Broad Bean Risotto

Description

This recipe creates a creamy tofu and beetroot pasta sauce. Recipe is for 2 people.

Ingredients
  • 3 beetroot
  • 2 blocks of firm tofu
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
Method
  1. Preheat the oven to 200c. Roughly chop the beetroot into cubes. Cover with oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cover the tofu with cayenne pepper, paprika, and salt and pepper.
  3. Place the tofu and beetroot in the oven. Remove the tofu after 30 minutes, and the beetroot after 45.
  4. Blend both together with the rest of the ingredients to make a lovely creamy sauce. Add water to change the consistency.
