Roast Lamb Shoulder

Description

This recipe creates a melt in the mouth lamb shoulder. Recipe is for 2 people. Make sure that you save the stock produced at the end!

Ingredients
  • 1KG bone-in lamb shoulder
  • 8 carrots
  • 2 onions
  • 1 head of garlic
Method
  1. Take the lamb out of the fridge half an hour before cooking. Cut the garlic head in half and rub over the meat. Cover the shoudler in salt.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200c. Roughly chop the carrots and onions, peel the garlic, and place them all at the bottom of a roasting tin.
  3. Place the lamb on top of the veg ans fill the tin with 2 pints of water. You are aiming to cover most of the lamb with this.
  4. Tent the tin with foil and put it in the oven for half an hour. When this half hour is up, turn the heat to 150c and cook for 3 hours.
  5. For the final half an hour, remove the foil to brown and crisp the lamb.
  6. When cooking is complete, take the lamb from the oven and let it rest for 15 minutes. No need to carve this meat, it should just fall off the bone.
  7. Serve with potatoes of your choice, the roasted veg, and a good serving of that stock.
